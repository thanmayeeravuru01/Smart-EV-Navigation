Maximize Your EV’s Efficiency ⚡

Simple habits that improve range, battery life, and charging efficiency.

🚗 Drive Smoothly

Smooth driving significantly improves range.

  • Accelerate gradually
  • Maintain steady speed
  • Avoid sudden braking
🛞 Monitor Tire Pressure

Under-inflated tires increase energy loss.

  • Check pressure monthly
  • Follow recommended PSI
  • Inspect before long trips
❄️ Limit AC & Heating

Climate systems consume significant battery.

  • Use eco climate mode
  • Pre-cool while charging
  • Prefer seat heaters
🔄 Use Regenerative Braking

Regenerative braking recovers lost energy.

  • Enable regen mode
  • Coast to slow down
  • Avoid harsh braking
🔋 Charge Between 20% – 80%

This range improves long-term battery health.

  • Avoid frequent 0–100% charging
  • Use fast charging only when needed
📍 Plan Charging Stops

Smart planning reduces anxiety and saves time.

  • Use ChargeMate navigation
  • Avoid peak congestion slots
  • Charge while shopping or resting
